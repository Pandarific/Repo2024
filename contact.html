Did you know?

  • Suzume’s Record-Breaking Success: His most recent film, Suzume (2022), became his highest-grossing work to date, earning over $300 million worldwide and securing Shinkai’s position as a leader in modern animation.

  • Delayed His Own Film Release: Shinkai once suggested that audiences shouldn’t expect Your Name. to surpass Miyazaki’s Spirited Away. He even asked theaters to stop screening the film in Japan when its popularity soared, as he felt it was overexposed.

  • A Cat Lover: Shinkai’s love for cats is evident in his work. His breakout short She and Her Cat is told from the perspective of a cat, and feline characters or motifs often make appearances in his films.

  • Language and Cultural Themes: Shinkai often incorporates Japanese culture and folklore into his work, such as the musubi (connections and threads of fate) concept in Your Name. and the mythological references in Children Who Chase Lost Voices.
